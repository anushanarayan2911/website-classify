Why we exist.
At Classify, we know how rigid, clunky, and robotic traditional student progress tracking can feel. That’s why we’ve set out to redefine what it means to meaningfully track student progress, whilst remaining effortlessly intuitive and easily adaptive to your unique feedback style.
Teachers and students deserve progress tracking that truly serves them.
That’s why we worked with primary and early year teachers from across  the country to create a platform that makes documenting student progress easier than ever.

Classify is simple to use, adaptive to your needs, and automatically sifts through student data to generate evidence-based, actionable insights  for you to take into the classroom.

That means you can get back to doing what you do best — teaching.
Every child counts.
We know there’s more to each child than a few rows on a spreadsheet or few ticked-boxes.

Classify is designed to be inclusive of every student’s progress journey. Our platform embraces factors such as SEND and EAL to generate realistic progress targets for each child.
