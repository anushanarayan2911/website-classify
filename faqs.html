Frequently asked questions
How can I record a reflection and do I have to do it every lesson?
It is as simple as pressing the record button,  and you can record reflections as often as you'd like! The more you do it, the more better your insights will be but it's entirely up to you.
How can I edit the reflection or change the classified level if I don’t agree with it?
All reflections, transcriptions and level determinations are fully editable. You have the final say to modify anything you see necessary.
How can I view progress of different groups of students?
You can analyse progress at a macro level by age, gender, cohort, subject, SEND, pupil premium and any other requirements on students' profiles.
How is student’s data kept safe?
Safety and privacy are our priority. We are GDPR compliant and with multiple levels of security, you can rest assured knowing that only your school can see students' data.
How can I set up and get training for staff to start using Classify?
We have no set-up fees, and we are here to help you transition platforms as seamlessly as possible. Get in touch to request training materials or book an online or in person training session.
